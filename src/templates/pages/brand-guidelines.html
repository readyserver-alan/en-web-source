{# Section Label #} Guidelines

Usage Guidelines

Best practices for using our brand assets

{# Do's #}

Do

  • Always maintain clear space around the logo
  • Use approved color variations only
  • Ensure proper contrast ratios (WCAG AA)
  • Use Inter font for all communications
{# Don'ts #}

Don't

  • Alter or distort the logo
  • Use unapproved colors or gradients
  • Place logo on busy backgrounds
  • Use logo smaller than minimum size (32px)

We only use and disclose Personal Data as permitted by the laws of Singapore and, where required, with your consent.

(a) To facilitate your access to and use of the Services, including but not limited to:

  • i. Creating your Account;
  • ii. Identification, due diligence and know-your-customer purposes;
  • iii. Maintaining and managing our relationship with you (e.g. notifying you of changes to the Terms of Use or this Privacy Policy);

  • v. Providing the Services to you;
  • vi. Providing customer support (e.g. troubleshooting issues with the VPS Services, Beta Services, Website or Mobile Application, responding to questions or feedback about products or services, etc.);
  • vii. Facilitating payment, which may also involve sharing your Personal Data to a third party (e.g. payment services gateway provider) where necessary to process payment transactions.

(b) To administer and protect the integrity of our business and the Services, the security of the Website and Mobile Application, including but not limited to:

  • i. Ensuring network security;
  • ii. Preventing or investigating any actual or suspected breaches of the Terms of Use or Privacy Policy, illegal activity, fraud, or other misconduct.

(c) To use data analytics to improve the Services, any of our other products and/or services and the customer experience, including but not limited to:

  • i. Tracking of visitor demographics, behaviour, activity and usage of the Services;
  • ii. Conducting user surveys and research;
  • iii. Understanding user preferences and usage in order to provide users with targeted or relevant information on the Website and/or Mobile Application;
  • iv. Improving and developing the Services or our products and/or services.

(d) To improve and develop the Services or our products and/or services generally;

(e) For marketing, promotion and advertising purposes, including but not limited to:

  • i. Sending you information about the Services, our business, products and/or services which we believe may be useful or relevant for you;
  • ii. Notifying you of promotions and discounts.

(f) For auditing of the Services or the business of Ready Server;

(g) In connection with any proposed transfer, purchase, merger and/or acquisition of all or any part of the business of Ready Server;

(h) For investigation of any actual or potential breaches of the Terms of Use or Privacy Policy;

(i) Any other purpose to which you have consented;

(j) Any other purposes which are permitted by law;

(k) To comply with any legal requirements or requests from government authorities; and

(l) Any other purposes which are incidental or related to the above.

If you have consented to us sending you marketing, promotion or advertising communications and provided us with your email address, mobile phone number or other contact details, we may send you such communications based on your indicated preference(s) (e.g. by email, SMS, WhatsApp, etc.).

You can choose not to receive such communications at any time, by indicating your preferences in your Account settings page.

You can choose not to receive such communications at any time, by disabling such features in your mobile device settings or the Mobile Application preference page, as may be applicable.

5. DISCLOSURES & TRANSFERS OF PERSONAL DATA

We may also disclose and/or transfer personal data to a third party to whom we may choose to sell, transfer or merge parts of or all of our business and/or assets; or if we choose to acquire or merge with other businesses. The new owners of our business may use your personal data in the same way as set out in this Privacy Policy.

We will not disclose or transfer your Personal Data outside Singapore except to jurisdictions which have data protection laws which offer at minimum an equivalent level of protection to Singapore law, or we will use tools to ensure that your Personal Data will be given the aforesaid level of protection (e.g. via contractual protections).

Except as provided above, we do not disclose and/or transfer Personal Data to any third party unless required by law.

We do not, and do not under any circumstances permit third parties to, monetise your Personal Data.

6. COOKIES

We deploy cookies or other similar technologies. These are small text files that we store on your browser or on your device. These enable us to recognise a particular browser or device, collect data for the purposes outlined in Section 3 (particularly Section 3(c)) and also to provide some of the Services.

You may manage and learn more about cookies by interacting with the cookie prompt on the Website. However, if you disable certain cookies which are required for certain functions of the Website, the availability of certain services or functions on the Website may be adversely affected.

7. DATA SECURITY

We have implemented security measures to protect Personal Data from accidental loss and unauthorised access or use. Additionally, access and use of your Personal Data is limited to our employees on a need-to-know basis and subject to confidentiality obligations.

Nevertheless, you must also implement reasonable security measures and practices to prevent accidental loss and unauthorised access to or use of your Personal Data, including the mandatory measures described in the Terms of Use and:

  • (a) Use a strong and unique password;
  • (b) Never share your password with anyone, including anyone from Ready Server;
  • (c) Implement good practices to prevent accidental discovery of your password by unauthorised parties (e.g., do not write your password down and leave it where others can view it);
  • (d) Limit access to your devices and browsers you use to access your account;
  • (e) Log out once you have finished using the Website and/or Mobile Application, especially if you are on a shared device.

8. RETENTION OF PERSONAL DATA

We retain personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes in Section 3, for any purposes permitted by law, and for the purposes of satisfying any legal, regulatory, tax, accounting or reporting requirements.

You can access and/or correct some of your Personal Data currently in our possession or control through the Mobile Application.

If you wish to access and/or correct your Personal Data that is not available through the Mobile Application (if any), you may request for access to and/or correction of your Personal Data that is within our possession or control by sending an email to our Data Protection Officer:

  • (a) In order to process your request, we may require you to provide additional information about your request, to verify your identity and entitlement to exercise access to or correct your Personal Data;
  • (b) We may charge a reasonable fee before we agree to process your request;
  • (c) We may refuse your request if it is unfounded, repetitive or excessive, if you refuse to pay the fee, or where we are otherwise permitted to do so by law;
  • (d) We will inform you within thirty (30) days of the time it will take to process your request.

10. WITHDRAWING CONSENT TO COLLECTION, USE AND/OR DISCLOSURE OF PERSONAL DATA

Where we rely on consent to collect, use and/or disclose your Personal Data, you can withdraw your consent at any time by sending an email to our Data Protection Officer:

  • (b) We typically process consent withdrawal requests within ten (10) business days. We will notify you if there are any delays or additional time required.

Please be aware that withdrawing your consent may mean that we may be unable to continue providing the Services to you and we may need to terminate your Account and/or other relationship with us.
